The Story of Behind Our Resort

In Streamedge Homestay accommodation is aesthetically designed with abundance of windows giving a good view of the surrounding lush green coffee plantation. We realize that our guests need more than serene surroundings & comfortable accommodation, so we invite you to discover the many local Malnad delicacies of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian prepared with organic and local ingredients & served with the best of hospitality from the hosts.

Come, immerse yourself in the authentic essence of Malnad hospitality at Streamedge Homestay, where every moment is a celebration of vibrant surroundings and flavorful culinary delights. From the moment you arrive, you'll be greeted with warmth and genuine hospitality, ensuring that your stay with us is nothing short of unforgettable. Indulge in a culinary journey that pays homage to the rich flavors of the land, prepared with care by our talented chefs. Whether you're exploring the lush surroundings or simply unwinding in your cozy accommodations, our dedicated team is here to ensure that your experience is filled with comfort, relaxation, and cherished memories.
