ABOUT ELEVEN

Welcome to Our Family

Eleven Guest House was born from a simple dream: to share the warmth and hospitality of Georgian culture with travelers from around the world. Founded by Koba and his family, our guest house represents the true spirit of Georgian hospitality - where every guest becomes part of our extended family.

Located in the historic heart of Kutaisi, our property has been lovingly transformed from a traditional Georgian home into a welcoming haven for travelers. We believe that the best way to experience Georgia is through authentic connections and genuine hospitality.

MEET OUR FAMILY

Koba & Family

Koba, our founder and host, embodies the true spirit of Georgian hospitality. With years of experience welcoming guests from around the world, he has learned to speak multiple languages to better serve our international visitors.

Our family takes pride in sharing Georgian culture through food, wine, and conversation. From our homemade wine made from grapes grown in our garden to traditional Georgian dishes prepared with love, every meal is an opportunity to share our heritage.
