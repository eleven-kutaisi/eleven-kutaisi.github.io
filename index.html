ABOUT ELEVEN

Authentic Georgian Hospitality

Located in the historic city of Kutaisi, Eleven Guest House offers a unique blend of traditional Georgian hospitality and modern comfort. Our family-run establishment provides an intimate experience where every guest becomes part of our extended family.

From our homemade wine to our traditional Georgian cuisine, every detail is crafted with love and authenticity. Experience the warmth of Georgian culture in our beautiful garden setting.
